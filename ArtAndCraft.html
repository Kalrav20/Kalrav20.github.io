Kaayakalp (Best Out of Waste)

1. Team event (2-4 members)

2. Participants have to carry their own materials.

3. Time – 1 hour 30 min

4. Event fee is Rs. 100/- per team.

5. Materials will NOT be provided at the spot.

6. ONLY waste material should be used. However, stationary items like Fevicol, Scissors, Stapler, Tape etc. are allowed.

7. The participants need to bring their own Waste Material. But use of partial, whole or readymade model and any other unfair means will directly lead to disqualification.

8. Theme: Open: Ability to develop & present in form of an object.

9. Presentation: Detail of the materials used and proper functional explanation of the design.

Drishyam (Graffiti)

1. The concept/ topic for the event is KHELEGA BHARAT, KHILEGA BHARAT

2. Team event (Max 5 members)

3. Event fee is Rs. 200/-per team.

4. Participants have to carry their own materials.

5. Do not bite (copy) someone else’s work. All graffiti must be completed on the wall area provided by us.

6. Time: 1.5 hours.

7. No naked or nude character on the wall. The crew violating this rule will be disqualified.

8. Similarly, no disparaging remarks on the wall would be allowed towards any political party.
