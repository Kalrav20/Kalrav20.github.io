FREESTYLE RAP BATTLE

A competitive event that provides rapper with opportunities to showcase their artistry and skills in spitting rhymes, for media exposure and championship titles.

1. The rapper must abide by the eligibility rules of Freestyle Rap Battle.

2. This is an individual event.

3. The rapper is required to complete the registration form and submit the registration form before the date of the competition.

4. Do not bite (copy) someone else work. Your freestyle must be 100% original material and shall not infringe upon any third party copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights nor shall it contain any vulgar words and sexually explicit lyrics.

5. By participating in this event you permit the recording of your contributions and assign to the organisers the complete copyright and all other rights in your contributions for use in all media.

6. Decision by the judges is final. There is no appeal process. PROTEST - are prohibited and will not be accepted regarding any score or result of a decision.

7. Competitors who are rude, abusive or deemed otherwise unsuitable by the organisers will be disqualified from the competition.

Battle of Bands

1. Band should have minimum 4 members and minimum 3 should play live instruments. Maximum 8 members are allowed . An assistant can be there for set-up and wind-up.

3. All bands will be given same stage and same lights .

4. All members of the particular band should be enrolled in same institute and must carry thier ID-Cards .
